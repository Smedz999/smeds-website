Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about residential air conditioning

How much does home air conditioning cost?

Costs vary depending on the size of your property and the number of rooms you want to cool. A single room system typically starts from £1,500, while multi-room systems range from £3,000 to £7,000. We offer free surveys to provide accurate quotes tailored to your specific needs.

Will installation damage my walls?

Our experienced engineers take great care during installation. We use minimal invasive techniques and always tidy up after ourselves. Most installations require only small holes for piping, which we seal professionally. We'll discuss the installation process during your free survey.

Are modern AC units noisy?

Not at all. Modern inverter air conditioners are whisper-quiet, operating at around 20-30 decibels—quieter than a library. You'll barely notice they're running, making them perfect for bedrooms and living areas.

Can AC units heat as well as cool?

Yes! Most modern systems are reverse-cycle heat pumps, meaning they provide both cooling in summer and efficient heating in winter. This makes them a cost-effective year-round climate solution for your home.

How long does installation take?

A single room unit can typically be installed in one day. Multi-room systems may take 2-3 days depending on complexity. We'll provide a clear timeline during your survey and always work efficiently to minimize disruption.
